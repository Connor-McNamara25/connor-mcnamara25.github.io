Intro

Hello! My name is Connor McNamara, and I use they/them pronouns. I am originally from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and now live in Somerville, Massachusetts. I have always been drawn towards computers, electronics, and anything technical in knowledge. The parts about programming and software development that draw me towards it are problem solving, creativity, and the nuance required to spot details that make the difference between good code and great code.

Thank you for taking a look at my portfolio. Here parts of my life are displayed, along with my coding, and my work history. My Linkedin can be found at here and my GitHub can be found here. My skill set involves Java, C++, HTML, CSS, Visual Basic, Python, and SQL.

Work

About

I love coding, hiking, photography, playing guitar and spending time with friends. My passion for coding stems from my involvement with electronics at a young age. Since I was a child, I have been around computers and that has drawn me to what makes them work and how we can configure them so they work best. This gave me a lot of experience with computer hardware, software, and processes in general. I also have taken pictures for art installations as photography is an interest of mine.
